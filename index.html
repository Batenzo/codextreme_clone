What is CodeXtreme?

CodeXtreme is a seasonal 4 days Hackathon that
brings together like-minded individuals from
all walks of tech to solve problems, network
with local and international tech experts and
companies, win prizes, and change lives with
their tech solutions.

Why should I join?

We give you the platform to learn from the best
in the industry. CodeXtreme is where “The
Rubber Meets The Road”. Come learn from other
hackers, build solutions with support fom our
mentors, win amazing prizes and share your
experience with the rest of the world.
